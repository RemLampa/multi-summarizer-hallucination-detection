Title: Rescue by the Carpathia

The RMS Carpathia arrived at the scene hours after the Titanic had sunk. Survivors in lifeboats were rescued from the freezing waters, many suffering from exposure and shock.

Captain Rostron of the Carpathia was praised for his swift response and careful navigation through dangerous ice fields. The rescue marked the end of a tragic night but the beginning of mourning for the many lives lost.
