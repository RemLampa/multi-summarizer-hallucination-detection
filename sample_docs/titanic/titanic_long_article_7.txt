Title: The Final Moments of the Titanic

In the final hours before the sinking, the ship’s bow sank deeper into the ocean as the stern rose high into the air. Passengers clung to hope, while others accepted the inevitable.

Eyewitnesses described the ship breaking apart before disappearing beneath the surface. The sounds of machinery and the cries of those left behind filled the night as the Titanic made its final descent.

Source: Survivor Accounts (Public Domain)
