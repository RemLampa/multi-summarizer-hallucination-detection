Title: Mixed Reactions to New Eco-Friendly Transport Plan

The announcement of a new eco-friendly transport system has sparked mixed reactions across the city. While environmental advocates applaud the initiative, some residents question its practicality.

The plan includes phasing out older vehicles and redesigning major roads to accommodate non-motorized transport. Critics argue that the city lacks the infrastructure readiness for such changes.

Officials have promised public consultations to address concerns before full implementation begins.
