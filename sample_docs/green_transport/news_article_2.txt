Title: Concerns Rise Over City's New Transport Overhaul

While city officials celebrate a new green transport plan, critics warn that the initiative may cause significant inconvenience for daily commuters. The proposal includes replacing traditional buses with electric fleets and reducing car lanes to prioritize cyclists.

Local business owners fear reduced accessibility could impact sales. "We support sustainability, but not at the cost of our livelihood," said one shop owner.

Authorities insist the long-term benefits will outweigh short-term challenges, but skepticism remains among residents.
