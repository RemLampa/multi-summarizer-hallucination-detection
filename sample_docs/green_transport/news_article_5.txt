Title: City Bets Big on Green Transport Future

The city is making a bold bet on sustainability with its newly announced transport overhaul. The initiative aims to modernize infrastructure while tackling climate change head-on.

Supporters see this as a necessary evolution for a growing urban center, while opponents worry about costs and execution risks.

Despite differing opinions, the plan marks a significant shift in policy direction, signaling the city's commitment to a greener future.
