Title: City Launches Ambitious Green Transport Plan

The city government today unveiled an ambitious green transport initiative aimed at reducing carbon emissions by 40% over the next decade. Officials highlighted investments in electric buses, expanded bike lanes, and pedestrian-friendly infrastructure.

Mayor Lina Cruz emphasized that the project is "not just about transportation, but about public health and the future of urban living." Environmental groups praised the move, though some commuters expressed concern about potential disruptions during implementation.

The program is expected to roll out in phases starting next year.
